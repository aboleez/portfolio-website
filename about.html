About Me

Hello! I’m Abolee


An artist/designer based in India, focused on UI design, UX research and Visual designing.


  • I love to paint, meeting and spending time with my close one’s is my way of relaxation.Apart from this, I enjoy listening to all kinds of music.
  • I have a background in interior design and graphic design, and I was eventually introduced to the concept of UI/UX design. Being an art and design student. I am well equipped with the knowledge of,design thinking and working process, the color theory and the concept of user-centric design.
  • I am a person who is inspired and finds comfort in things that are minimal yet functional.And always aim to design products that are pretty and easy to understand.
  • Brainstorming and planning out the perfect user flows have always been my favorite part of the designing process,and for me, it is like a half battle won. Since this is one of the most essential milestone to understand the working of an app or a website.
  • Thus, I aim to define, design and deliver interesting, organized and simple solutions to my users.
